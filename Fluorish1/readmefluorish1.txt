“City of Seattle Wage Data.” Seattle.gov, 3 Mar. 2017, data.seattle.gov/City-Administration/City-of-Seattle-Wage-Data/2khk-5ukd/about_data.

The visualization I created shows the average hourly wage per department of city of Seattle.
The data was grouped by department and then averaged to compare pay levels across Seattle's 
varying departments running things like transit, police, mayoral office, etc. This data set
originates from City of Seattle's Open Data portal which provides publicly available data
representing different city data.
